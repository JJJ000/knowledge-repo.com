## Float
Float is a single-precision 32-bit IEEE 754 floating-point datatype. It has a range of approximately 1.4E-45 to 3.4E+38 and is represented by the keyword `float`.

## Double
Double is a double-precision 64-bit IEEE 754 floating-point datatype. It has a range of approximately 4.9E-324 to 1.8E+308 and is represented by the keyword `double`.
