### ScreenX/Y
ScreenX/Y refer to the coordinates of the mouse relative to the top-left corner of the user's display.

### ClientX/Y
ClientX/Y refer to the coordinates of the mouse relative to the top-left corner of the browser window.

### PageX/Y
PageX/Y refer to the coordinates of the mouse relative to the top-left corner of the document.

### Difference
The main difference between these three is that ScreenX/Y refer to the user's display, ClientX/Y refer to the browser window, and PageX/Y refer to the document.
