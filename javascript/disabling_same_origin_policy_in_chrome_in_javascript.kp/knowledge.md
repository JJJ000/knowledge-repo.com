##### Disabling the Same Origin Policy in Chrome

1. Go to the Chrome menu, then select **Settings**.
2. In the **Privacy and Security** section, click **Site Settings**.
3. Scroll down and click **Additional**.
4. Under **Privacy and Security**, select **Allow sites to access your local resources**.
5. Toggle the switch to **Allow**.
