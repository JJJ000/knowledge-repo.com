### Deleting the Forked Repository

1. Go to the forked repository page on GitHub.
2. Click on the **Settings** tab.
3. Scroll down to the **Danger Zone** section.
4. Click on the **Delete this repository** button.
5. Confirm the action by typing the name of the repository.
6. Click on the **I understand the consequences, delete this repository** button.

### Deleting the Fork from Your Profile

1. Go to your profile page on GitHub.
2. Click on the **Repositories** tab.
3. Locate the forked repository and click on the **Delete** button.
4. Confirm the action by typing the name of the repository.
5. Click on the **Delete repository** button.
